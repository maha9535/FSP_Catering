Frequently Asked Questions

What sorts of birthday parties do you host? +

We accommodate diverse birthday parties, including children's birthdays, milestone events such as 18th or 50th birthdays, and small family reunions.

Do you have vegetarian and non-vegetarian options? +

Gokula Catering specializes exclusively in high-quality vegetarian cuisine, offering a wide range of traditional and contemporary dishes.

Do you provide themed catering for birthdays? +

Yes, we can tailor our menu and presentation to match your chosen birthday theme, ensuring a cohesive and festive experience.

Are there cake and dessert alternatives available? +

Absolutely! We offer a variety of dessert options including traditional sweets, pastries, and can coordinate special birthday cake requirements.

When is the best time to schedule your services? +

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability and proper planning for your special day.

Can you manage big parties? +

Yes, we are equipped to handle birthday celebrations ranging from intimate gatherings of 50 people to large-scale events with over 1000 guests.
