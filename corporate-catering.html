Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you cater for all types of corporate catering? +

Yes, we take orders for conferences, seminars, office parties, product launches, team lunches and more.

2. Do you only offer Vegetarian dishes? +

Yes, we specialize in pure vegetarian catering, offering a vast array of South Indian, North Indian, and International vegetarian delicacies.

3. Do you offer menu options? +

We provide multiple menu tiers and fully customizable options to fit the theme and budget of your corporate event.
