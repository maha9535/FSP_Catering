Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of food are available for weddings? +

South Indian, North Indian, Continental, Chinese, and fusion cuisines are among the many cuisines Gokula Catering will provide. You may alter our menus to fit your tastes.

Are you able to accommodate certain dietary needs? +

Yes, we specialize in addressing various dietary requirements including gluten-free, vegan, nut-free, and other specific preferences to ensure all your guests enjoy the meal safely.

Are there live food counters available? +

Absolutely! We provide live counters for Dosa, Pasta, Chaat, and various starters to add an interactive and fresh element to your wedding feast.

How far in advance should I book Gokula Catering services? +

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for wedding season dates to ensure availability and proper planning for your special day.

Are you capable of managing big weddings? +

Yes, Gokula Catering has the capacity and experience to handle events ranging from intimate gatherings to massive weddings with several thousand guests.
