Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which meals are available for Seemantham Sapadu? +

Traditional Tamil vegetarian foods such as a variety of rice, sambar, rasam, kootu, poriyal, vadai, payasam, and more are available at our catering services. It is also possible to alter menus to include particular foods according to your tastes.

2. Do you cater the Bang Ceremony or Valaikappu? +

Yes, we specialize in Valaikappu (Bangle Ceremony) catering, ensuring all traditional ritual requirements and specific food types mandated for the ceremony are provided.

3. Can I change the Seemantham catering menu? +

Absolutely! We offer flexible menu customization. You can choose from our standard Seemantham packages or create a unique menu that fits your family traditions.

4. Do you provide food for parties in Seemantham? +

Yes, we provide catering for all associated celebrations, including small home gatherings and large party hall events for Seemantham.

5. How many guests can you accommodate at a Seemantham event? +

We can cater for intimate groups of 30-50 guests up to large ceremonial gatherings with over 500+ guests.

6. When is the best time to reserve your Seemantham catering services? +

We recommend booking at least 10-15 days in advance to ensure the best seasonal ingredients and proper ceremonial arrangements.
